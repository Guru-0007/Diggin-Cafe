All About Diggin

Where Good Vibes
Meet Great Food

Step into Diggin — an escape wrapped in fairy lights, lush greens, and the warmth of a European café. Every corner is made for good vibes, easy conversations, and a little magic.

Our kitchen brings you comforting Italian and continental classics — handcrafted pastas, stone-baked pizzas, and indulgent desserts made to linger over. From Anand Lok to Connaught Place, each Diggin is a destination.
